### English

**MailLighter** helps you send lighter, cleaner emails directly from Outlook.

With one click from the ribbon, MailLighter removes the unnecessary bulk that accumulates in email threads : embedded images, file attachments, layers of quoted replies. Your recipients get only what matters.

---

**Why MailLighter?**

Every time you reply to a long thread, Outlook drags along everything that came before : quoted signatures, embedded logos, files already shared, stacked replies going back weeks. MailLighter gives you fast, targeted controls to trim exactly what you want before hitting Send.

But beyond convenience, there's a real environmental cost to heavy emails. Every megabyte you send travels through data centers, gets stored on servers, and is replicated across backup systems — all of which consume electricity and raw materials. A single email with a large attachment, forwarded across a team and kept in dozens of inboxes, quietly generates a small but real carbon footprint. Multiply that by thousands of emails per day across an organization, and the impact adds up.

MailLighter helps reduce that footprint at the source, before you hit Send. Lighter emails mean less storage consumed on mail servers, less bandwidth used in transit, and less energy spent keeping that data alive across data centers. It's a small habit that, scaled across a team or a company, contributes to a measurably lighter digital footprint.

---

**What you can do:**

🖼️ **Remove Images**
Strip all embedded images from the email body. MailLighter tells you how many images were removed and how much space was saved.

📎 **Remove Attachments**
Remove file attachments from your message. Only real files are removed ; inline images used as signatures or logos are left untouched.

↩️ **Keep 2 Replies**
Trim long reply chains down to the 2 most recent messages. Works across multiple email formats and languages — MailLighter intelligently detects quoted blocks whether they use Outlook's standard formatting, border dividers, horizontal rules, or plain-text headers.

✂️ **Keep Selection Only**
Selected a passage in the email body? Replace the entire quoted thread with just that selection. Ideal when you want to keep one specific excerpt and nothing else.

✅ **Full Cleanup**
Run all cleanup actions at once. Remove images, attachments, and reply trimming in a single click. A summary notification shows exactly what was removed and how much space was freed.

---

**Key benefits:**

- **Faster**: All actions available in a single dropdown menu added to the Outlook ribbon
- **Transparent**: Each action reports what was removed and the space saved (KB, MB, or GB)
- **Non-destructive**: Only the current draft is modified ; original emails in your inbox are never touched
- **Multilingual**: Full support for English, French, and Spanish
- **Privacy-first and GDPR compliant**: All processing happens locally inside Outlook ; no data is sent to external servers
- **Eco-friendly**: Reduce your digital footprint by sending lighter emails

---

**Requirements:**

- Microsoft Outlook for Windows or macOS (desktop)
- Microsoft 365 or Exchange account

## Politique de confidentialité — points clés

- Tout le traitement s'effectue localement dans Outlook via l'API Office.js
- Aucune donnée n'est transmise à des serveurs tiers
- L'add-in n'accède qu'au message en cours de composition (permission `ReadWriteItem`)
- Aucun compte utilisateur, aucune collecte d'email, aucune analyse de vos données
